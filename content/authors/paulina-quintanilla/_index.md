Paulina is an Assistant Professor (UK Lecturer) in Process Systems Engineering in the Department of Chemical Engineering at UCL, and leads the LOOPS research group. She is also an Honorary Lecturer at the Sargent Centre for Process Systems Engineering, Imperial College London, and an Early Career Advisory Board member for *Control Engineering Practice* (IFAC) and *Minerals Engineering* journals, and a Scientific Editor for *Results in Engineering* (Elsevier).
